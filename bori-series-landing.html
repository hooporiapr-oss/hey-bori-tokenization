This Is Not A Podcast.
It Starts The Conversation.

BoriCast is built for youth basketball players who are already training their game — but still need to understand the business side of basketball. Each short episode ends with a direct question that pulls the player into a real conversation with Bori.

Three Levels.
One Player Mindset System.

The first 30 episodes are organized to move a player from awareness, to separation, to long-term mastery.

Level 1: Awareness

Episodes 001–010 wake players up to value, reputation, influence, discipline, opportunity, and productivity.

Level 2: Separation

Episodes 011–020 help players understand role, consistency, coachability, time, weaknesses, and readiness.

Level 3: Mastery

Episodes 021–030 build longevity, pressure control, emotional control, self-awareness, failure response, and purpose.

The Format Is Simple.
The Impact Is Different.

A Host Bot asks the question. Bori answers, challenges the player’s thinking, and ends by inviting the player into a real conversation. No scoring. No judging. Just awareness.

1. Listen

The player hears a short basketball-business conversation that feels real and direct.

2. Reflect

Each episode ends with one personal question the player cannot ignore.

3. Talk

The player chooses a response and Bori continues the conversation.
